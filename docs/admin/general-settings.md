# General Settings

Go to **Company Settings > General** to configure company-wide settings. This section has three tabs.

## Branding

Customise your company's appearance in SIMPLOGIX:

- **Company logo** -- drag and drop or click to upload your logo (PNG, JPG, WebP, or SVG, up to 5 MB)
- **Company name** -- your organisation's display name
- **Primary colour** and **Secondary colour** -- choose brand colours using the colour picker or enter a hex code. A live preview shows both swatches.

## Localisation

Set defaults for how dates, times, and numbers are displayed:

| Setting | Options |
|---|---|
| **Timezone** | Select from all standard timezones |
| **Date format** | YYYY-MM-DD, DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, and more |
| **Time format** | 12-hour or 24-hour |
| **Number format** | Locale-based (e.g. 1,000.00 or 1.000,00) |
| **Currency** | Select your currency |
| **Default language** | Application language |

A live preview box shows how the current date, time, a sample number, and a currency value look with your selected settings.

## Working hours

Define your standard working hours for each day of the week:

- Set **start time** and **end time** for each day
- Toggle individual days on or off
- Use **Apply to Weekdays** to copy Monday's hours to all weekdays
- Use **Apply to All** to copy Monday's hours to every day

A weekly summary shows total working days and hours per week.
