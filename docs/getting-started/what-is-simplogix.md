# What is SIMPLOGIX?

SIMPLOGIX is an industrial asset management platform designed to help teams track assets, run inspections, and manage defects — all in one place.

## What you can do with SIMPLOGIX

- **Manage assets** — Keep a centralised register of all your equipment and infrastructure
- **Run inspections** — Record inspection results against assets in the field
- **Track defects** — Log, assign, and resolve defects found during inspections or day-to-day operations
- **Monitor your site** — Get a real-time view of asset health and outstanding issues across your site

## Who uses SIMPLOGIX?

SIMPLOGIX is built for industrial and operational teams, including:

- **Site administrators** who manage users, assets, and site configuration
- **Managers** who need visibility over inspections and defect status
- **Operators** who carry out inspections and log defects in the field
- **Viewers** who need read-only access to reports and asset data
