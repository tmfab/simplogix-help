# Navigating the App

## The dashboard

After logging in you'll land on the **Dashboard**. This gives you a high-level summary of your site's current status — open defects, recent inspections, and asset counts.

## The sidebar

The left-hand sidebar is your main navigation. From here you can access:

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of site activity |
| **Assets** | View and manage your asset register |
| **Inspections** | Run and review inspections |
| **Defects** | Log and track defects |

## Switching sites

If you have access to more than one site, use the **site selector** at the top of the sidebar to switch between them. All data shown is scoped to the currently selected site.

## Your account

Click your name or avatar in the top-right corner to:

- View your profile
- Change your password
- Sign out
