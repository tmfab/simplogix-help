# Logging In

## Accessing SIMPLOGIX

Open your browser and go to [app.simplogix.app](https://app.simplogix.app).

## Signing in

1. Enter your **email address** and **password**
2. Click **Sign in**

If you don't have an account, contact your site administrator — they can invite you from the user management section.

## Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes)
4. Follow the link to set a new password

## Trouble logging in?

- Make sure you're using the email address your account was created with
- Passwords are case-sensitive
- If your account has been deactivated, contact your site administrator
