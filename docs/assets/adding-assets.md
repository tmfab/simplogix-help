# Adding Assets

Admins and Managers can add new assets to the register.

## Steps

1. Go to **Assets** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add asset** (top right)
3. Fill in the asset details:
   - **Name** — a clear, identifiable name for the asset
   - **Asset ID** — your internal reference number or tag
   - **Type** — the category of asset (e.g. pump, vehicle, structure)
   - **Location** — where the asset is located on site
   - **Status** — active, inactive, or decommissioned
4. Click **Save**

The asset will now appear in your asset register and can be selected when running inspections or logging defects.
