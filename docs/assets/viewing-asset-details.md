# Viewing Asset Details

Click any asset in the asset list to open its detail page.

## What you'll see

### Asset information

The top of the page shows the asset's core details -- name, display ID, asset type, plant area, status, and criticality. Any custom template fields configured for your site are also displayed here.

### Health and metrics tiles

Below the asset details you'll find summary tiles showing:

- **Health score** -- an overall condition indicator based on recent inspections and defects
- **Defect trend** -- a visual summary of defect activity over time
- **Inspection compliance** -- whether inspections are being completed on schedule

### Reference photos

A photo gallery showing all reference photos attached to the asset. Click a photo to view it full-size.

### Inspections tab

A list of all inspections carried out on this asset, sorted by date. Click any inspection to view its full record.

### Defects tab

All defects currently or previously logged against this asset. Click any defect to open its detail page.

## Editing an asset

Managers and Admins can edit asset details by clicking **Edit** on the asset detail page. This opens the same three-tab wizard used during creation, with the current values pre-filled.

## Decommissioning an asset

If an asset is no longer in service, set its status to **Decommissioned** rather than deleting it. This preserves its inspection and defect history for record-keeping and compliance.

## Deleting an asset

Only Admins can delete assets. Deletion is permanent -- consider decommissioning instead to preserve the audit trail.
