# Viewing Asset Details

Click any asset in the asset list to open its detail page.

## What you'll see

- **Asset information** — name, ID, type, location, and status
- **Inspection history** — a list of all past inspections carried out on this asset
- **Open defects** — any defects currently logged against this asset

## Editing an asset

Admins and Managers can edit asset details by clicking **Edit** on the asset detail page. Update the relevant fields and click **Save**.

## Decommissioning an asset

If an asset is no longer in service, set its status to **Decommissioned** rather than deleting it. This preserves its inspection and defect history for record-keeping.
