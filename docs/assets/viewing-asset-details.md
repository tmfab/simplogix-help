# Viewing Asset Details

Click any asset in the list to open its detail page.

## Asset information

The top of the page shows the core details: name, display ID, asset type, plant area, status, and criticality. Any custom template fields configured for your site appear here too.

## Health and metrics tiles

Below the asset details, summary tiles show:

- **Health score** — an overall condition indicator based on recent inspections and defects
- **Defect trend** — a visual summary of defect activity over time
- **Inspection compliance** — whether inspections are being completed on schedule

## Reference photos

A photo gallery of all reference photos attached to the asset. Click any photo to view it full-size.

## Inspections tab

All inspections carried out on this asset, sorted by date. Click any inspection to view its full record.

## Defects tab

All defects currently or previously logged against this asset. Click any defect to open its detail page.

## Editing

Managers and Admins can edit asset details by clicking **Edit** on the detail page. This opens the same three-tab wizard used during creation, with current values pre-filled.

## Decommissioning

If an asset is no longer in service, set its status to **Decommissioned** rather than deleting it. This preserves the inspection and defect history for record-keeping and compliance.

## Deleting

Managers and Admins can delete assets. Deletion is permanent — consider decommissioning instead to preserve the audit trail.
