# Assets

The **Assets** section is your site's centralised register of equipment, infrastructure, and anything else you want to track.

## What is an asset?

An asset is any physical item you want to inspect or monitor — a pump, a vehicle, a piece of electrical equipment, a structure, or any other item relevant to your operations.

## The asset list

The assets page shows all assets registered to your current site. You can:

- **Search** by asset name or ID
- **Filter** by asset type or status
- **Click any asset** to view its full details, inspection history, and open defects
