# Inspections

Inspections let you record the condition of assets at a point in time. Every inspection is tied to a specific asset and creates a permanent record.

## Why inspections matter

Regular inspections help you:

- Catch issues before they become serious problems
- Maintain a compliance trail for auditing and reporting
- Track asset condition over time

## The inspections list

The inspections page shows all inspections carried out at your current site. You can filter by asset, date range, or status to find what you're looking for.
