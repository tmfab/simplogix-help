# Viewing Inspection History

Every inspection is stored permanently and can be reviewed at any time.

## From the inspections list

Go to **Inspections** and use the list, Kanban, or calendar view to find inspections:

- **List view** -- filter by status, asset, plant area, date range, or inspector. Customise which columns are visible. Click any row to view the full record.
- **Kanban view** -- see inspections grouped by status (Overdue, Due Soon, Scheduled, Completed). Click any card to view details.
- **Calendar view** -- browse inspections by date. Click a day to see that day's inspections, or use the upcoming panel to find overdue and upcoming items.

## From an asset's detail page

Open any asset and go to the **Inspections tab** to see all inspections carried out on that asset, sorted by date. Click any inspection to view the full record.

## What an inspection record shows

Each completed inspection record includes:

- **Inspector name** and **completion date**
- **Overall result** -- Pass or Fail
- **Checklist responses** -- every item with its pass/fail/N/A status and any notes
- **Photos** captured during the inspection
- **Linked defects** -- any defects created from failed checklist items
- **Signature** from the inspector

## Assigning inspections

Managers can reassign inspections to different operators:

- From the **list view** -- use the row action menu
- From the **calendar view** -- use the inline inspector selector on each card
- From the **inspection detail page** -- click the assignee to change it
