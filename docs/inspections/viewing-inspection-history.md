# Viewing Inspection History

Every inspection is stored permanently and can be reviewed at any time.

## From the inspections list

Go to **Inspections** and use the filters to find inspections by:

- **Asset** — see all inspections for a specific asset
- **Date range** — view inspections carried out in a given period
- **Inspector** — filter by who carried out the inspection

Click any inspection to view the full record including checklist results and notes.

## From an asset's detail page

Open any asset and scroll to **Inspection history** to see all inspections carried out on that asset in chronological order.
