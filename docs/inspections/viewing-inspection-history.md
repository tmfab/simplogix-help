# Viewing Inspection History

Every inspection is stored permanently and can be reviewed at any time.

## From the inspections list

Go to **Inspections** and use whichever view suits you:

- **List view** — filter by status, asset, plant area, date range, or inspector. Customise which columns are visible. Click any row to view the full record.
- **Kanban view** — inspections grouped by status (Overdue, Due Soon, Scheduled, Completed). Click any card for details.
- **Calendar view** — browse by date. Click a day to see that day's inspections, or use the upcoming panel to find overdue items.

## From an asset's detail page

Open any asset and go to the **Inspections tab** to see all inspections for that asset, sorted by date.

## What an inspection record shows

Each completed inspection includes:

- Inspector name and completion date
- Overall result (Pass or Fail)
- Every checklist response with its pass/fail/N/A status and any notes
- Photos captured during the inspection
- Linked defects created from failed items
- The inspector's signature

## Assigning inspections

Managers can reassign inspections to different operators:

- From the list view, use the row action menu
- From the calendar view, use the inline inspector selector on each card
- From the inspection detail page, click the assignee to change it
