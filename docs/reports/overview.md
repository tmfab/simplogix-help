# Reports

The **Reports** section gives you pre-built reports, a custom report builder, and scheduled exports.

## Pre-built reports

Select a report type from the tabs at the top of the page. All reports respect your current site selection and date range.

| Report | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Compliance rate, risk distribution, inspection completion by week, defect status breakdown, asset status, inspector performance |
| **Defect Analysis** | Defect trends, severity breakdown, and detailed metrics |
| **Inspection Trends** | Completion rates and trends over time |
| **Risk Matrix** | Risk trend by month, risk by plant area, average days open per risk level, new vs. closed high-risk defects |
| **Corrective Actions** | Repair pipeline stages, MTTR trend, overdue defects by risk level, on-time vs. late closeout, deferred backlog |
| **Asset Health** | Average health score, assets below 50%, critical overdue count, worst-performing assets, condition trends, inspection compliance by type |
| **Safety and WHS** | High safety risk count, hazard permits, restricted area verification status, safety trends, high-risk plant areas |
| **Executive Summary** | KPI cards with traffic-light indicators and trend direction, risk comparison vs. previous period, top critical issues |
| **Workforce** | Inspector count, average completion hours, completion rate, inspector performance with workload levels |
| **Compliance** | Schedule adherence, mandatory overdue inspections, data retention status |

## Date range

Use the date range picker at the top of the page to filter all reports to a specific time window.

## Exporting reports

Click **Export Report** to download the current report:

| Format | Best for |
|---|---|
| **CSV** | Importing into spreadsheets |
| **PDF** | Formatted report with charts, suitable for sharing or printing |
| **Excel (XLSX)** | Multiple sheets with formatting and formulas |

## Custom reports

The **Custom Reports** tab lets you build your own:

1. Choose a **data source** — Assets, Defects, or Inspections
2. Select **dimensions** — what to group by (e.g. plant area, status, month, asset type)
3. Select **metrics** — what to measure (e.g. count, average risk score, total cost)
4. Add **filters** using a visual query builder (field, operator, value with AND/OR logic)
5. Choose a **visualisation** — Table, Bar chart, Pie chart, Line chart, or Area chart
6. Click **Run Report**

You can save configurations as templates for reuse and share them with your site.

## Scheduled reports

The **Scheduled** tab lets you set up recurring exports:

- Choose data type, format (CSV, XLSX, or JSON), and frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly)
- Set delivery time and timezone
- Reports run automatically and are available for download

Use the action menu on each schedule to edit, enable/disable, run immediately, or view run history.
