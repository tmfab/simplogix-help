# Managing Defects

Admins and Managers can update and resolve defects.

## Updating a defect

1. Open the defect from the defects list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update the status, add notes, or reassign it
4. Click **Save**

## Resolving a defect

When a defect has been fixed:

1. Open the defect
2. Change the status to **Resolved**
3. Add a **resolution note** describing what was done
4. Click **Save**

Resolved defects remain in the system for audit and reporting purposes.

## Assigning a defect

To assign a defect to a team member:

1. Open the defect
2. Click the **Assignee** field and select a user
3. Save — the assignee will be able to see defects assigned to them from their dashboard

## Filtering and prioritising

Use the defects list filters to focus on what matters:

- Filter by **severity** to address critical issues first
- Filter by **asset** to see all issues for a specific piece of equipment
- Filter by **assignee** to manage workloads across your team
